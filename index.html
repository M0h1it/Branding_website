Our Heritage

Serving Families Since 1995

For over 30 years, Star Halal Meat Shop has been the trusted choice for families in Gurugram, delivering excellence and tradition in every cut. What started as a small family butcher shop has grown into a symbol of trust.

We believe that good food starts with the best ingredients. That's why we source our livestock directly from trusted farms, ensuring that every piece of meat is 100% Halal, fresh, and free from preservatives.
